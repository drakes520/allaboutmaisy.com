All about Maisy

Information

Maisy is a border-collie golden retriever cross. She is highly energetic out of the house but extremely calm at home.She especially dislikes, fireworks, cats, and lime scooters. She loves meeting new people, and dogs. Her favourite place in the world is the beach.

Maisy loves trips in the car and especiallly loves speedbumps. Sometimes when the boot is left open she hops in and won't exit unless fed dinner. Maisy's favorite time of day is dinnertime Maisy normally has dog roll and biscuits for dinner, but ocassionaly fests on bones, pumpkin, or apple.

A very social dog Maisy often enjoys watching movies with the family on the couch. Maisy loves playing fetch but doesn't like to drop the ball often turning the game into chase. Maisy loves walks and runs and after every run hops into her paddling pool in the backyard. Sometimes maisy enjoys a salad on her walk often eating long grass.
