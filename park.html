Information

Maisy is a border-collie golden retriever cross. She is highly energetic and loves going to the park.

Some of Maisy's favorite parks

  • Avonhead park
  • Burnside park
  • The Groynes dog park
  • Frosbie park

Maisy loves meeting new dogs and chasing the ball when at the park. If there is a pond or stream she will hop in, and go for a swim. If there is anyone playing with a ball from humans to dogs she has to chase them and attempt to steal their ball.
